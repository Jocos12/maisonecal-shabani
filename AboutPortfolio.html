About Ghislain Kisamba:

Ghislain is a logistics professional with a degree in International Solidarity Logistics from the Bioforce Institute in France.

In addition, he is certified in Management and Leadership, as well as in Humanitarian Project Design and Management, all obtained in France.

His impressive professional career spans over 8 years as a logistics manager, where he has offered his expertise to the Norwegian Refugee Council Mission in Niger, Cameroon, the DRC, and other countries.

As a logistician, Ghislain demonstrates exceptional management of complexity, essential for coordinating logistics operations in sometimes challenging environments.

He shows great flexibility to quickly adapt to unforeseen situations and emergencies, a crucial skill in the humanitarian field.

His sharp organizational skills allow him to efficiently manage stocks, supplies, and a human resources team while maintaining the necessary structure.

Ghislain is also an effective communicator, able to coordinate with other teams, partners, and agencies to ensure the success of operations.

His analytical skills enable him to make informed decisions by examining data, costs, deadlines, and risks. In addition, he effectively manages the stress associated with emergency situations, which is essential for maintaining operational stability.

Armed with a solid technical knowledge of logistics tools, supply chain management systems, and regulations, Ghislain ensures that operations run smoothly.

He pays meticulous attention to details to avoid costly mistakes, thus demonstrating his commitment to excellence in his field.

Finally, Ghislain maintains a strict adherence to ethics, essential for managing the valuable resources dedicated to humanitarian missions.

His strong educational background and impressive career as a logistics coordinator attest to his exceptional contribution to the success of humanitarian operations in various African countries.
